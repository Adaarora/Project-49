Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of iguana suborder.
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among Chameleons.
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational activities.
  3. Pigeons have excellent hearing ability.

Interesting Facts about Camel

  1. There are 2 types of camels:-One humped/Dromedary camels and two humped/Bacterian camels.
  2. Camel's hump can store 80 pounds of fat and they can live off of it for weeks and even months.
  3. Camels can travel upto 40 miles per hour.

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  1. Lifespan of a cicada is 17 years.
  2. They can be found on any continent except Antartica.
  3. They prune mature trees.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  1. Adult pandas are good at climbing trees.
  2. Pandas can have 12 to 35 kgs of bamboo per day.
  3. Pandas are lazy.
